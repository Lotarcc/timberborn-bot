# Factions: Folktails vs Iron Teeth

## Head-to-head
axis | Folktails (F) | Iron Teeth (IT)
motto | "Comfort, food, sturdy wood" — farming focus | "Work hard" — industry/efficiency, ignores environment
water pump | Water Pump (depth ≤2) + Large Water Pump / Badwater Rig (more space+labor efficient) | Deep Water Pump (deeper reservoirs) + Deep Badwater Pump
base housing | Lodge cap 3 (12 logs); Double 6 / Triple 9 need SP | Barrack cap 10 (40 logs); Large Barrack 16; Rowhouse 5
breeding | inside Lodges: ≥2 adults, no critical need, in one dwelling; pause Lodges to cap pop | Breeding Pod (10 logs) — pop scales w/ #pods × avg wellbeing; decoupled from housing
food | "expert farmers": Efficient Farmhouse, Beehive, Aquatic Farmhouse; processing simple, only Gristmill needs power | Hydroponic Garden (no irrigation, compact, stackable) but needs steady water + food industry
power | strong early Water Wheels; Wind Turbine + Gravity Battery (no labor/fuel mid-game) | Large Power Wheel early; Steam Engine (logs→power); Industrial Lumber Mill (2× planks, +50% power); Badwater Discharge
unique | Timberbots (goods-fed), Zipline Stations, Contamination Barriers, Underground Piles | Deep pumps, Hydroponics, Steam Engine, Control Tower, badwater industry
badwater | avoid it; can be contaminated (ailment) | tolerant — pumps & uses badwater as a resource
beginner | EASIER: cheap log buildings, simple food, forgiving | harder: bigger buildings, food processing chains, badwater management

## Shared (both factions)
- Same needs/wellbeing model, same Inventor (1 SP/h), Campfire, tanks, dams/levees/floodgates, District Center, Path (free).

RULE: default to Folktails for the autonomous run — cheaper (mostly logs), simpler food, breeding tied to housing (easy pop control).
RULE: if F — pump with Water Pump (river depth ≤2), house in Lodge (cap 3), farm carrots in Efficient Farmhouse, control pop by pausing/building Lodges.
RULE: if IT — pump with Deep Water Pump (reach deep water), house in Barrack (cap 10, few buildings), breed via Breeding Pods (build/pause pods to set pop), plan a food-processing chain early (hydroponics need water + industry).
RULE: if IT — keep wellbeing HIGH: it directly raises breeding rate, not just productivity.
RULE: IT can pump/use badwater; F cannot safely — never route badwater near Folktails beavers (contamination -10 WB).
